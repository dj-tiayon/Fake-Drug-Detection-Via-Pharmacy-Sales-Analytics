# Fake-Drug-Detection-Via-Pharmacy-Sales

This project analyzes pharmacy sales transactions to identify suspicious drug distribution patterns that may indicate counterfeit or substandard medicines within the pharmaceutical supply chain in Nigeria. Using Excel, SQL, and Power BI, the analysis focuses on detecting anomalies related to pricing, drug quantities, expiry conditions, unknown brands, and supplier behavior.

The dashboard was designed to support data-driven decision-making by helping stakeholders:
Identify suspicious drug transactions
Detect high-risk suppliers and pharmacies
Monitor unusual pricing and sales patterns
Improve counterfeit drug surveillance
Support public health monitoring and regulatory actions
